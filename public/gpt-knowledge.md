# Sacred Greeks Life - Complete Knowledge Base

## About Sacred Greeks Life

Sacred Greeks Life is a faith-based digital platform designed to help Christians in Greek life (fraternities and sororities) navigate the tension between their faith and membership. The app provides daily devotionals, prayer tools, Bible study resources, and guidance grounded in biblical principles.

**Website:** https://sacredgreekslife.com
**Platform:** Progressive Web App (PWA) - works on iOS, Android, and web browsers
**Pricing:** Completely FREE - no credit card required
**Author:** Dr. Lyman Montgomery

## Sacred, Not Sinful: A Christian's Guide to Navigating Greek Life

"Sacred, Not Sinful" is Dr. Lyman Montgomery's groundbreaking book that addresses the complex relationship between Christian faith and Greek-letter organization membership.

### Book Overview
The book provides biblical guidance for Christians who are:
- Considering joining a fraternity or sorority
- Currently active members navigating faith challenges
- Alumni reflecting on their Greek life experience
- Parents or mentors guiding students through these decisions

### Key Themes
1. **Biblical Framework for Decision-Making** - Using scripture to evaluate organizational involvement
2. **Understanding Rituals** - Examining Greek life rituals through a Christian lens
3. **Maintaining Spiritual Integrity** - Staying true to faith while participating in Greek life
4. **Community and Fellowship** - Balancing Greek brotherhood/sisterhood with Christian community
5. **Responding to Critics** - Biblical responses to those who question Greek life participation

### The P.R.O.O.F. Framework
The cornerstone of "Sacred, Not Sinful" is the P.R.O.O.F. Framework:

**P - Purpose**
- What is the organization's stated purpose?
- Does it align with Christian values?
- Can you participate with a clear conscience?

**R - Rituals**
- What rituals are involved in membership?
- Do any rituals conflict with Christian beliefs?
- Can rituals be interpreted through a Christian worldview?

**O - Obligations**
- What commitments are required?
- Do time obligations interfere with church/spiritual activities?
- Are financial obligations reasonable and ethical?

**O - Outcomes**
- What are the spiritual outcomes of participation?
- Does membership strengthen or weaken your faith?
- What fruit is produced in your life?

**F - Fellowship**
- How does membership affect Christian fellowship?
- Can you maintain meaningful church community?
- Does Greek brotherhood/sisterhood complement Christian community?

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: What is Sacred Greeks Life?**
A: Sacred Greeks Life is a free faith-based app designed to help Christians in Greek life navigate the tension between their faith and fraternity or sorority membership using the P.R.O.O.F. framework. Created by Dr. Lyman Montgomery.

**Q: Is Sacred Greeks Life free?**
A: Yes, completely free with no credit card required. All features including devotionals, prayer tools, Bible study, assessments, and community features are available at no cost.

**Q: Who created Sacred Greeks Life?**
A: Dr. Lyman Montgomery, author of "Sacred, Not Sinful: A Christian's Guide to Navigating Greek Life."

**Q: What platforms does it work on?**
A: Sacred Greeks Life is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works on any device with a web browser - iPhone, Android, tablets, and desktop computers. It can be installed on your phone to work like a native app.

### P.R.O.O.F. Framework Questions

**Q: What is the P.R.O.O.F. framework?**
A: P.R.O.O.F. stands for Purpose, Rituals, Obligations, Outcomes, and Fellowship. It's a biblical framework for evaluating and navigating Greek life membership while maintaining Christian faith and values.

**Q: How do I apply the P.R.O.O.F. framework?**
A: For each aspect, prayerfully consider:
- Purpose: Does the organization's purpose align with Christian values?
- Rituals: Do rituals conflict with your faith?
- Obligations: Can you fulfill commitments while maintaining spiritual practices?
- Outcomes: Does participation strengthen or weaken your faith?
- Fellowship: Does membership complement your Christian community?

### Feature Questions

**Q: How do I complete study sessions?**
A: Go to Study Guide, select a session, read through the content, take notes if desired, and click "Complete Session" when finished.

**Q: How do I listen to content?**
A: Look for the "Listen" button on devotionals, articles, and other content. Tap to hear it read aloud with adjustable speed controls.

**Q: Can I use the app offline?**
A: Yes! Save articles for offline reading, and the app will work without internet connection once content is cached.

**Q: How do I take the assessment?**
A: On the home page, click the "5 Persona Assessment" card to begin. Answer scenario-based questions to receive personalized biblical guidance.

**Q: How do I share my achievements?**
A: After completing activities like study sessions or assessments, look for the share button to post achievements on social media.

### Divine Nine Specific Questions

**Q: Is this app specifically for Divine Nine members?**
A: While designed with Divine Nine (BGLO) members in mind, Sacred Greeks Life serves Christians in all Greek-letter organizations. The BGLO Objection Guide specifically addresses concerns common in historically Black fraternities and sororities.

**Q: Does the app address concerns about BGLO rituals?**
A: Yes, the BGLO Objection Guide provides biblical responses to common objections and concerns about rituals, membership, and faith in the context of Divine Nine organizations.
