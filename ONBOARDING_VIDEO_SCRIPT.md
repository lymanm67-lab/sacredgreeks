# Sacred Greeks Onboarding Video Script (30 Seconds)

## Scene Breakdown

### Opening (0-5 seconds)
**Visual**: Sacred Greeks logo with animated gradient
**Voice**: "Welcome to Sacred Greeks—your daily companion for navigating Greek life with faith."

### Daily Tools (5-10 seconds)
**Visual**: Quick montage showing devotional page, prayer journal, Bible study
**Voice**: "Get daily devotionals, prayer guides, and Bible study tools to strengthen your spiritual walk."

### Community Features (10-15 seconds)
**Visual**: Prayer Wall with requests, community service tracker
**Voice**: "Connect with your brothers and sisters—share prayer requests and track service together."

### Growth & Progress (15-22 seconds)
**Visual**: Progress dashboard with achievements, streaks, P.R.O.O.F. assessment
**Voice**: "Track your spiritual journey, earn achievements, and navigate tough decisions with the P.R.O.O.F. framework."

### Closing (22-30 seconds)
**Visual**: Dashboard overview, then fade to "Get Started" button
**Voice**: "Everything you need to live out your faith in Greek life, all in one place. Let's get started!"

---

## Production Notes

- **Tone**: Warm, encouraging, confident
- **Voice**: Young adult, conversational (similar to a friend introducing you)
- **Music**: Subtle, uplifting background track (no lyrics)
- **Pace**: Fast-paced but clear, matching Gen Z attention span
- **Text Overlays**: Key phrases appear as captions for accessibility
  - "Daily Devotionals"
  - "Prayer & Community"
  - "Track Your Growth"
  - "P.R.O.O.F. Framework"

## Screen Recording Setup

**Software Recommendations**:
- Loom (for quick screen recordings)
- Screen Studio (for polished, animated recordings)
- ScreenFlow or Camtasia (for professional editing)

**Recording Tips**:
1. Use clean test account with sample data
2. Enable smooth scrolling and animations
3. Record at 1920x1080 minimum resolution
4. Use cursor highlighting for user guidance
5. Add smooth transitions between features
